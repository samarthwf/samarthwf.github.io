About Us

Samarth Welfare Foundation is a registered non-profit on a mission to transform hardship into hope. We empower vulnerable communities across Maharashtra through free health services, women’s skill development, education for orphaned children, and more.

What We Do

  • 📍 Free health camps for cancer, heart disease, diabetes, and TB
  • 👩‍🏫 Vocational training centers for women
  • 📚 Education & digital literacy for orphans and underprivileged children
  • 🧠 Mental health and de-addiction support programs
  • 🌿 Environment care through tree plantation & cleanliness drives
  • 🧑‍⚕️ Youth training in technical & paramedical skills
