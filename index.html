About Us

Samarth Welfare Foundation is a registered charitable trust committed to uplifting the marginalized through healthcare, education, skill development, and social empowerment across Mumbai and Maharashtra. We believe that every life deserves dignity and opportunity.

Our Work

  • Organizing free health, cancer, diabetes, and wellness camps
  • Vocational training for underprivileged women
  • Education support for orphaned and disabled children
  • Youth skill development in technical and paramedical fields
  • Running de-addiction and mental wellness programs
  • Promoting computer literacy among women and orphans
  • Tree plantation drives and cleanliness awareness programs
